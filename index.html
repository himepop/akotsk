Rules

Generate a 3×3 card, pick a ship or fandom, and write one fic per square.

1. Free Space

The center square is already complete. Use it as a wildcard, author’s choice, or bonus fill.

2. One Line Bingo

Complete any row, column, or diagonal to get bingo.

3. Full Card

Complete all nine squares for full bingo.

4. Mixing Prompts

You can combine multiple squares in one fic, or write a separate fic for each square.

5. Tag Kindly

Use appropriate AO3 tags, ratings, warnings, and author notes for each fill.
